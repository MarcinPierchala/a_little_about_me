Bee Treatment Device

Varroa is a growing threat to bees. Therefore, I designed and made a device for analyzing atmospheric conditions inside the hive.

The device also has the function of precise drug dosing. Currently, the amount of the drug is not related to the conditions inside the hive,

but the aim of this project is to find out the relationship between the temperature and humidity in the hive and the quality of bee treatment given the dose of the drug.

In the future, it will be possible to fully automate this process and ensure the safety of the bees during the treatment process.
